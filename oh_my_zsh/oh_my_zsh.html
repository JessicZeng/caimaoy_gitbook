插件

  • zsh-syntax-highlighting 设置命令正确绿色高亮,错误红色高亮
    安装
    $ git clone git://github.com/jimmijj/zsh-syntax-highlighting ~/.oh-my-zsh/custom/plugins/zsh-syntax-highlighting
    然后在~/.zshrc中插件那添加plugins=(zsh-syntax-highlighting)
